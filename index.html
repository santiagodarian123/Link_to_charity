Shop to give

At no cost to you. We are a not-for-profit initiative that makes everyday online shopping a simple way to give back. When you shop through our website links, Link to Charity earns a commission and donates 100% of it after minor operating costs directly to trusted charities supporting environmental, humanitarian, and animal welfare, completely for free.

For an even easier experience, download the official Link to Charity Google Chrome extension to donate automatically while you shop.

Support Charities

By using our links, and purchasing from your favourite websites, you help raise money for charities dedicated to improving lives and environments worldwide. This is achieved through affiliate programs that pay us a certain portion of your purchase cost that we can then donate. By doing this we can help people, to help others, with all the money we receive going directly to charity or supporting this movement through ways of advertising, staff wages and other costs such as website hosting and domain registration.
